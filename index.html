Our Expertise

Our core team brings more than 15 years of international trade and investment expertise, each member having served over a decade at Fortune 500 companies. Our Core Businesses

  • Commodity Trading: Specializing in bulk commodities such as sugar, soybeans, corn, edible oils, and mineral ores (including cobalt, copper, molybdenum). Brosper actively sources from key resource-exporting countries across Africa and South America.
  • Cobalt Sourcing: Leveraging deep and longstanding relationships, Brosper delivers ethically sourced, high-quality cobalt from the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) to European and global customers, ensuring consistent quality and competitive advantage.
  • Investment Advisory (M&A): Providing strategic advisory for cross-border mergers and acquisitions, particularly in sectors involving natural resources, commodities, and industrial operations. Brosper’s extensive industry experience facilitates informed and impactful investment decisions.
